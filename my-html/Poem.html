Life is a river, flowing free,
winding through each mystery.
With calm and storm, it finds its way,
from golden dawn to end of day.
It teaches lessons, soft and deep,
in moments loud, and those we keep.
Through every loss and every gain,
it whispers growth behind the pain.
It blooms in joy, it bends in strife,
each breath a thread that weaves a life.
So walk the path with open eyes,
and greet the world beneath the skies.
