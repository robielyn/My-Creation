Clover Kingdom

Hello, I am Rovelyn Paradero

I was born under mysterious circumstances and raised in the shadows of the Kingdom. Trained in Illusion Magic, I showed a rare talent for creating lifelike illusions from a young age.

As an outcast, I found strength in solitude. My shadow-based illusions now serve as tools for deception and reconnaissance, making me a valuable asset in any mission.

Though quiet and distant, I’m fiercely loyal to those I trust. I struggle to balance my true self with the expectations of the Magic Knights.

My greatest challenge lies within — mastering the dark edge of my power and forging my own destiny, no longer living in the shadow of others.
